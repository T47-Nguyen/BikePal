LESS THAN $10 A MONTH!

MORE FLEXIBILITY, MORE RIDE TIME

72 HOUR ACCESS PASS $15

VISITORS AND CITY EXPLORERS

The perfect pass for those who plan on taking full advantage of the bike-share system. Purchase a 3-Day Pass for $15 for 72-hour access to unlimited 30-minute rides.

The first 30 minutes of each ride are included in the base price of the pass. After 30 minutes extra usage fees apply, see Overage Fees below.

24 HOUR ACCESS PASS $7

SHORT STAYS AND OCCASIONAL RIDERS

It’s a short-term commitment that provides flexibility to those looking for a one-day adventure. Purchase a Day Pass for $7 for 24-hour access to unlimited 30-minute rides.

The first 30 minutes of each ride are included in the base price of the pass. After 30 minutes extra usage fees apply, see Overage Fees below.

SINGLE TRIP FOR $3.25

QUICK AND EFFICIENT ONE-WAY TRIP

Purchase a Single Trip for $3.25 for a one-way trip up to 30 minutes in length.

Trips lasting longer than 30 minutes will incur extra usage fees apply, see Overage Fees below.

OVERAGE FEES: $4 FOR EVERY EXTRA 30 MINUTES

For every trip exceeding 30 minutes (or 45 minutes with the Annual 45 Membership), overage fees will accumulate at a rate of $4 for each (up to) 30 minutes of additional trip time. To avoid an overage charge, before your 30 minute (or 45 minute) trip is up, dock the bike and unlock another to continue riding.
