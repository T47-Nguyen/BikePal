It's Greener on Our Side of the Road

Back in the day, donkeys were a common and democratic form of transport – that everyone could get around on.

With BikePal growing herd of bikes across Europe and beyond, we are working to get people to join the republic and be a part of democratizing green mobility, to not only transform cities, but lives.

Today more than 70% of Europeans live in cities and therefore transforming the way we move is critical in achieving a sustainable future. Basically there are 4 major areas that bike sharing has a positive impact on:

  • Improving public health
  • Minimising emissions
  • Reducing congestion
  • Better use of public space

    • Why every Ride counts

    --our impacts in number

    We mean it when we say that every ride counts. Here you see calculations of the positive impacts of a biking versus taking a car, train or e-scooter. The calculations are based on BikePal's study that we commissioned Dresden University to do in Copenhagen during October-November 2020. The study focused on what influence bike sharing has on our travel behaviour and what replacing for example a car trip with a bike trip means in terms of savings within: health benefits, congestion and CO2.
