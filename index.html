HOW TO BUY SHORT TERM PASSES

LESS THAN $10 A MONTH!

Short-term passes are perfect for visitors, occasional riders and city explorers. There are three short-term pass options available:

  • $3.25 Single Trip (One-way trip)
  • $7 Day Pass (24-hour access)
  • $15 3-Day Pass (72-hour access)

    • Enjoy unlimited 30-minute, station to station trips while your pass is active. Short term passes can be purchased in-person at a Bike Share Toronto kiosk or on your smartphone using PBSC, the official app of Bike Share Toronto.
